# Privacy Policy for Bubble Pop

**Last Updated: May 15, 2026**

## Introduction

Bubble Pop ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mindfulness and breathing exercise app.

## Information Collection and Use

### Information We DO NOT Collect

Bubble Pop is designed with privacy as a priority. We do NOT:

- Collect any personal information
- Track your location
- Access your contacts
- Access your photos
- Collect usage analytics
- Share data with third parties
- Sell your data

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following preferences are stored locally on your device only:

- App settings (sound enabled/disabled, haptic feedback preferences)
- Selected visual theme
- Selected breathing pattern
- Tutorial completion status
- Session statistics (total sessions, total bubbles popped)

This data never leaves your device and is only used to personalize your app experience.

### Subscription Information

If you purchase a premium subscription:

- All subscription transactions are processed through Apple's App Store
- We do not store or have access to your payment information
- Apple handles all payment processing securely
- Your subscription status is managed through your Apple ID

## Data Storage

- All app preferences are stored locally using iOS UserDefaults
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No cloud storage is used
- Data remains on your device until you delete the app

## Third-Party Services

### Apple App Store

When you purchase a subscription, Apple's App Store handles:
- Payment processing
- Subscription management
- Purchase receipts

Apple's privacy policy applies to these transactions:  
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

### No Other Third Parties

Bubble Pop does not integrate with any other third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

Bubble Pop does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is designed to be safe for all ages, with no data collection, no social features, and no external links (except for support and privacy policy).

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no data to:
- Access
- Modify
- Delete
- Export

Your app preferences stored locally can be cleared by:
1. Deleting the app from your device
2. Resetting your iOS device

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Posting the new policy in the app
- Notifying users through the App Store update notes (for significant changes)

## Data Security

Your locally stored preferences are protected by:
- iOS system-level security
- Device passcode/biometric authentication
- No transmission over networks
- No external server access

## International Users

Bubble Pop can be used worldwide. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no international data transfer concerns.

## California Privacy Rights

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):
- We do not collect, sell, or share personal information
- There is no data to access, delete, or opt-out of
- The app fully complies with CCPA requirements

## Summary

**In Plain English:**

Bubble Pop is a simple, private mindfulness app that:
- ✅ Stores your preferences only on your device
- ✅ Never sends your data anywhere
- ✅ Doesn't track you
- ✅ Doesn't sell your information
- ✅ Uses Apple's secure payment system for subscriptions

Your mindfulness practice is personal, and we respect that.
